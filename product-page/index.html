Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of a laser cutter for sheet metal?

It is designed to cut various types of sheet metal with precision, allowing for intricate designs and shapes that are essential in manufacturing processes.

What are the benefits of using aluminum tubing in manufacturing?

Aluminum tubing offers an excellent strength-to-weight ratio, high corrosion resistance, and great workability making it ideal for various structural parts.

How is aluminum tubing produced?

It is mostly produced through an extrusion process where heated aluminum is forced through a die to shape it into continuous tubes.

What are the common applications of aluminum tubing?

Common applications include aerospace frames, scaffolding, heat exchangers, and automotive components.

Can aluminum tubing be customized?

Yes, aluminum tubing can easily be cut, bent, welded, and finished to specific custom dimensions and aesthetics.
